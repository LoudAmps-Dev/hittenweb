The Band

Hard N' Heavy
Machine

"What if you threw all your favorite 80s Hard Rock bands into a blender and added a spicy dose of classic Heavy Metal? That's HITTEN."

A true Hard N' Heavy machine. The band blends arena-sized hooks, screaming twin guitars, powerful vocals, and a fierce live energy that brings the golden era of Rock into the present day — 'Best Of Both Worlds' as Van Halen said.

Over the past decade, HITTEN has taken their show across Europe and beyond — including a successful US tour and two sold-out nights in Tokyo. Their explosive live energy has earned them spots at major festivals such as Wacken Open Air and Rock Imperium.
